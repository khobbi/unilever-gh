Welcome to Unilever Ghana - Enhancing Lives, Every Day

At Unilever Ghana, we are committed to making sustainable living commonplace. Since our establishment in 1992, with a legacy dating back to 1928, we have been a trusted name in nutrition, hygiene, personal care, and well-being.

Our diverse portfolio of brands, including Lipton, Omo, Lux, Vaseline, and Pepsodent, touches millions of lives across Ghana, providing high-quality products that help people look good, feel good, and get more out of life.

Driven by innovation and sustainability, we continue to create solutions that meet the evolving needs of our customers. Explore our food, home care, personal care, and beauty products-crafted to bring convenience, health, and confidence to every home.

Join us on our journey to a better, more sustainable future for all.
